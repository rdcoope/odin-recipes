Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Meat (optional)
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water (approximately 3+ cups) to a boil
  2. Put in noodles
  3. Boil noodles for about 10 minutes
  4. Drain noodles into strainer (or collander)
  5. In a seperate pan, cook up the meat
  6. While the water is waiting to boil, mix the sauce into the meat and place on low to simmer
  7. Combine and enjoy. Be careful as the pasta may still be hot
